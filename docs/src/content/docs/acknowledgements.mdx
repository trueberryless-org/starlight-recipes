The Starlight Recipes plugin stands on the shoulders of giants. I want to express my deepest gratitude and credit the individuals who pour their passion and effort into the Astro and Starlight ecosystems. Their dedication makes this community better every day, and this project would not be possible without their groundwork.

## Special Thanks

A massive thank you goes to **[HiDeoo](https://github.com/HiDeoo)**. This project owes much of its existence to their incredible work on the **[Starlight Yeoman Plugin Generator](https://github.com/HiDeoo/generator-starlight-plugin)** and the **[Starlight Blog](https://github.com/HiDeoo/starlight-blog)**.

I spent a significant amount of time analyzing the Starlight Blog codebase, adopting its best practices and architectural patterns to ensure this plugin reached the same level of quality. Beyond the code, I am particularly grateful for HiDeoo's willingness to share knowledge; their guidance on complex concepts, such as adapting Starlight’s Route Data into a custom "Recipes Data" structure, was instrumental in bringing this plugin to life.

## General Thank You

I also want to extend my sincere thanks to the entire **[Starlight](https://github.com/withastro/starlight)** team and their wonderful **[contributors](https://github.com/withastro/starlight/graphs/contributors)**. You have built a world-class foundation for documentation.

Finally, thanks to the users of Starlight Recipes. Your feedback and ideas are essential to the project's growth. If you have suggestions or encounter any bugs, please feel free to **[submit an issue or feedback on GitHub](https://github.com/trueberryless-org/starlight-recipes/issues/new/choose)**.
