The Starlight Recipes plugin stands on the shoulders of giants. I want to express my deepest gratitude and credit the individuals who pour their passion and effort into the Astro and Starlight ecosystems. Their dedication improves this community every day, and this project would not be possible without their groundwork.

## Special Thanks

A massive thank you goes to **[HiDeoo](https://github.com/HiDeoo)**. This project owes much of its existence to their incredible work on the **[Starlight Yeoman Plugin Generator](https://github.com/HiDeoo/generator-starlight-plugin)** and the **[Starlight Blog](https://github.com/HiDeoo/starlight-blog)**.

I spent a significant amount of time analyzing the Starlight Blog codebase, adopting its best practices and architectural patterns to ensure this plugin reached the same level of quality. Beyond the code, I am particularly grateful for HiDeoo's willingness to share knowledge; their guidance on complex concepts, such as adapting Starlight’s Route Data into a custom "Recipes Data" structure, was instrumental in bringing this plugin to life.

## Users

The growth of Starlight Recipes is fueled by the community that uses it. Your feedback, ideas, and real-world use cases are essential to the project's evolution, and I am sincerely grateful for your support. If you have suggestions or encounter any bugs, please feel free to **[submit an issue or feedback on GitHub](https://github.com/trueberryless-org/starlight-recipes/issues/new/choose)**.

## Contributors

To all who have contributed their time, code, and creativity to this repository: thank you for being an essential part of this journey. Every pull request, bug report, and thoughtful suggestion has directly shaped Starlight Recipes into a more robust tool for the community. Your willingness to share your expertise not only improves the software but also fosters a collaborative environment that keeps open-source development vibrant and inclusive. I am truly grateful for your hard work and for the unique perspectives you bring to this project.
