Serve the semolina porridge immediately in deep bowls while it is still warm. Generously dust the top with a mixture of **cinnamon and sugar**. To match the styling in the photo, arrange a handful of **fresh blueberries and raspberries** in the center, and add a **sprig of fresh mint** for a pop of green. If you're feeling indulgent, sprinkle over some _grated chocolate_ or _ground hazelnuts_ for extra texture and flavor.
